Human

Humans are versatile and ambitious, embodying a wide range of skills and personalities. Their adaptability and drive allow them to excel in various environments and roles across the galaxy. Known for their diverse cultures and ingenuity, humans are natural leaders, explorers, and innovators. With a knack for quick learning and problem-solving, they thrive in almost any circumstance, making them a prominent presence in the galactic community.

Human Traits

Ability Scores

All ability scores increase by 1.

Age

Humans live to around 100 years old.

Alignment

Humans tend towards lawful alignments.

Size

Humans are on average 2 meters tall.

Movement

Humans have a 30ft movement speed.

Languages

Humans can speak Basic and two additional languages of your choice.

Human Variant

Increase any two ability scores of your choice by 1 each. Also gain one feat of your choice at 1st level.
