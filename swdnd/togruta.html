Togruta

Togrutas are a peaceful and communal species, often found living in close-knit societies on their lush homeworld of Shili. Renowned for their agility and keen awareness, Togrutas rely on their natural echolocation abilities, provided by their distinctive montrals, to navigate and sense their surroundings with precision.

Togruta Traits

Ability Scores

Your Wisdome score increases by 2, and your intelligence score increases by 1.

Age

Togruta live for around 80-90 years.

Alignment

Togrutas are a peacful speicies, known for being good alignments

Size

Togruta are around 5-6ft in height.

Movement

Togruta have a movement speed of 30ft.

Languages

Togruta can speak Basic and one other language of your choice.

Echolocation

Togrutas have Montrals that serve as an auditory system that allows them to sense movement of objects around them though echolocation. Gain proficiency in insight and perception, if you already have proficiency gain expertise.

Agility

You gain proficiency in Acrobatics and Athletic checks.
