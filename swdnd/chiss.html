Chiss

Mysterious and strategic, the Chiss are a disciplined and highly intelligent species known for their striking blue skin and glowing red eyes. Originating from the Unknown Regions, they possess remarkable reaction times and heightened perception. With a natural affinity for order and strategy, Chiss make exceptional leaders and tacticians.

Chiss Traits

Ability Scores

Your intelligence increases by 2, and your wisdom by 1

Age

Chiss are considered adults at 15 and typically live 100 years.

Alignment

Chiss favor order, and as such, tend to be lawful alignments.

Size

Chiss are average height, they typically stand at 2 meters.

Movement

Chiss have 35 meters of movement speed.

Languages

Chiss speak Baisc and one other language of your choice

Infrared vision

Chiss can see into the infrared spectrum and have Darkvision.

Reaction Time

Chiss have one extra reaction per combat round. This reaction can only be used to dodge an attack.
