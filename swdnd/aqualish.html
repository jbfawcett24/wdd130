Aqualish

Aqualish are a hardy and temperamental species, known for their rugged physiques and strong personalities. Often quick to anger, they can be formidable allies or fierce opponents. Their sturdy builds and natural endurance make them well-suited for life in harsh environments. Standing tall and broad, they are a presence that commands attention. Aqualish are also known for their adaptability, thriving in a variety of roles, from laborers to warriors, and their keen eyesight allows them to excel even in the dimmest conditions. Their bold nature and boisterous demeanor make them unforgettable in any setting.
