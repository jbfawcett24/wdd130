Gungan

Gungans are resilient and agile beings with strong ties to their aquatic environments. Their amphibious nature allows them to thrive both on land and underwater, making them adaptable explorers. Known for their powerful legs, Gungans can leap impressive distances with ease, showcasing their natural athleticism.
