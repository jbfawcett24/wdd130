Droid Traits

Ability Scores

You can increase one ability of your choice by 2, and another by 1

Age

Droids live forever, assuming they can get proper care and upkeep.

Alignment

Droids favor no alignment.

Size

You can choose your size, either small or medium

Movement

Droids have a movement speed of 35 feet

Languages

Droids speak Basic, Binary, and one other language of your choice. They can download new languages

Efficient Recharge Cycle

Droids only need a 4 hour long rest rather than 8 hours.

Self-Repair Protocols

Droids cannot heal stim packs, however three times per short rest they can make repairs on themselves healing 1d4.
