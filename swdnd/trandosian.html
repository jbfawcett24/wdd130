Trandosian

Trandoshans are towering, reptilian hunters known for their immense strength and resilience. Their society revolves around honor in the hunt, often leading them toward ruthless or self-serving pursuits. With keen survival instincts and a long lifespan, Trandoshans excel in harsh environments and thrive in combat. Their natural darkvision and physical prowess make them formidable and fearsome beings across the galaxy.

Trandosian Traits

Ability Scores

Age

Trandosians can live for around 200 years.

Alignment

Trandosians tend towards Evil alignments.

Size

Trandosians are around 3 meters tall.

Movement

Trandosians have a movement speed of 35ft.

Languages

Trandosians can speak Basic, Huttese, and one other language of your choice.
