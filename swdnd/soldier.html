Soldier

Trained for battle and forged in the crucible of war, soldiers are the backbone of any fighting force. With grit, discipline, and unwavering resolve, they excel in the heat of combat, wielding a variety of weapons with precision and adaptability. Whether holding the line, leading the charge, or rallying allies in the face of overwhelming odds, soldiers stand as pillars of strength on the battlefield.

Hit points

Hit Dice: 1d8
Hit Points at 1st level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Soldier level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: light armor, medium armor
Weapons: Blasters, Vibroblades, Explosives
Tools: Engineering kit
Saving throws: Strength, Constitution
Skills: Athletics, Survival, Nature, Pick 1 from: Sleight of Hand, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Perception, Stealth

Equipment

2 Dc-17 blaster pistols or a Dc-15a blaster rifle. A vibrosword, and a basic med kit.

Ability Score Improvments

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2 or increase 2 ability scores by one. You cannot increase a skill above 20.

Skills

Level 3: Take Aim

You take aim at your target, giving you an advantage on your next attack. 2 uses per short rest.

Level 5: Med Pack

Heals target 2d8 health. 3 uses per short rest. This ability upgrades at levels 7, 13 and 19. At each of these levels, add 2 uses (per short rest) and increase the amount healed by 2d8. At level 19, once per long rest, you may choose to spend 3 Med kits to fully heal an ally, as well as add 1d20 temp hit points to their total.

Level 5: Rapid Fire

Once per short rest, rapidly fire 3 shots at nearby enemies (or all at one) each dealing the weapon's damage.

Level 7: Suppressive fire

Gives enemy disadvantage on all attacks on this character for 3 turns. Does 1d12 damage on use.

Level 9: Power Shot

Once per short rest, roll one extra dice for your damage roll.

Level 9: Extra Attack

When you take the attack action, you may make one additional attack.

Level 12: Reinforcements

Once per long rest, as an action, call in 3 soldiers with 20 hp, 13 AC, and armed with a blaster that deals 1d8 damage. Once the combat encounter is over, they leave.

Level 15: Orbital Strike

Call in reinforcements, dropping 3 bombs and dealing 3d12 explosive damage in a 5 ft range.

Level 17: Heavy Forces

Once per long rest, as an action, call in a tank with 125 hp, 22 AC, and a cannon that deals 4d12 explosive damage. Only usable inside of combat.

Level 20: Motivation

Once per short rest, give yourself an extra attack and double the damage of your attacks for 2 turns.
