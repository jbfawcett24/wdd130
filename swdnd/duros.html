Duros

Hailing from a proud lineage of explorers, Duros are natural wanderers, often charting new stars or navigating bustling trade routes. Their keen intellect and nimble reflexes make them adept in both science and survival. With a talent for adapting to new challenges, Duros thrive in the ever-changing expanse of the galaxy, embodying the spirit of discovery.
