Nightsister

Mystics of the dark arts, Nightsisters harness the ancient magick of Dathomir to bend reality to their will. Using their mystical powers, they craft spectral weapons, mend wounds, summon undead warriors, and teleport across the battlefield with eerie grace. Masters of manipulation and resilience, Nightsisters strike fear into the hearts of their enemies while bolstering their allies with otherworldly strength.

Hit points

Hit Dice: 1d8
Hit Points at 1st level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Mandalorian level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light Armor
Weapons: All Melee weapons, light blasters
Tools:
Saving throws: Dexterity, Wisdom
Skills: Choose 4 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, Perception, Religion, Survival, Sleight of Hand

Equipment

A Vibroblade, a vial of Spirit Ichor, 100 Credits

Ability Score Improvments

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2 or increase 2 ability scores by one. You cannot increase a skill above 20.

Skills

Some Nightsister abilities require a Magick roll or a Magick save. The bonus to you Magick rolls is your wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus. The Magick save is 8 + your wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus.

Level 3: Dark Healing

as a Bonus Action:
You heal a target you can see for 2d6 health, while you take half of that as necrotic damage, Or
You heal yourself for 2d6 health, and a willing target you can see takes half of that as necrotic damage.

Level 5: Teleportation

You or a target you touch teleports up to 30 feet away where you can see.

Level 7: Magick Weapon

You summon a Magick sword or bow. the weapon deals 2d6 necrotic damage on hit, and has a +2 enchantment. This ability lasts for 10 turns. You can use this ability as many times as equal to your proficiency bonus per short rest.

Level 9: Drain Life

as an Action, make a Magick attack roll against an anemy you can see within 60 feet. On a hit, deal 4d8 necrotic damage and heal yourself for that amount. You can use this ability as many times as equal to your proficiency bonus per short rest.

Level 12: Magick Strength

You or a willing target you touch increase your strength and constitution to 23 for 1 hour. This ability can be used twice per long rest.

Level 15: Confusion

All enemies wihtin 30 feet of you must make a wisdom save (Magick save DC). On a fail, they become confused. A confused target can't take reactions and must roll a d10 at the start of each of its turns to determine its behavior for that turn.
1: The creature uses all its movement to move in a random direction. To determine the direction, roll a d8 and assign a direction to each die face. The creature doesn't take an action this turn.
2-6: The creature doesn't move or take actions this turn.
7-8: The creature uses its action to make a melee attack against a randomly determined creature within its reach. If there is no creature within its reach, the creature does nothing this turn.
9-10: The creature can act and move normally.

Level 17: Lighting

Chose up to 5 targets within 30 feet of you and make a Magick attack roll. On a hit the target takes 6d10 necrotic damage. You can use this ability as many times as equal to your proficiency bonus per short rest.

Level 20: Chant of Resurection

You create 5 zombies at spaces you can see withing 30 ft. These zombies cannot be killed for 20 mintues after this ability is used.
The zombies have 16 strength, and 10 in all other stats.
They have thier own initiative, and on thier turn can attack for 2d6 + strength. You can use this ability 2 times per long rest
