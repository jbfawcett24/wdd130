Pilot

Masters of the stars and the vessels that traverse them, pilots are daring adventurers who thrive in the chaos of space. Whether deftly evading enemy fire, executing impossible maneuvers, or navigating treacherous hyperspace routes, they embody the spirit of exploration and ingenuity. With their hands on the controls and their eyes on the horizon, pilots carve paths through the galaxy that few would dare to follow.

Hit points

Hit Dice: 1d8
Hit Points at 1st level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Hit points at Higher Levels: 1d8 (or 5) + your Constitution modifier per Pilot level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light Armor
Weapons: All Blasters, Vibroblades
Tools: Engineering Kit
Saving throws: Strength, Dexterity
Skills: Sleight of hand, Survival, Piloting, Pick 1 from: Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Perception, Stealth

Equipment

A small ship, an E-5 blaster rifle. Choose a starship repair kit with 5 uses, or a small droid. The droid cannot be sold or used in combat.

Ability Score Improvments

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2 or increase 2 ability scores by one. You cannot increase a skill above 20.

Skills

Level 3: Confusing the Enemy (Starship)

Gives enemy disadvantage attack rolls on you for the next 2 turns.

Level 3: Pilot Archetype

At level 3 you chose a specialization in piloting skill. You can choose smuggler or starship pilot. You gain new abilities through this archetype at level 3, 7, 12, and 19.

Level 5: Crash Landing (Starship)

Allows you to try to land a crashing ship [Reduce dc to 13 Piloting]

Level 7: Pilots Intuition (Either)

Once per short rest, you can gain advantage on an initiative check. At level 11, you gain the alert feat.

Level 9: Shoot from the Hip (Ground)

Once per short rest, as a bonus action, rapidly fire 3 shots at nearby enemies (or all at one) each dealing the weapon's damage. You add the damage modifier once for each target.

Level 12: Multi shot (Starship)

Once per short rest, roll one extra dice for your damage roll

Level 15: Sharpshooter (Starship)

Once per short rest, double the attack roll on your shot.

Smuggler Archetype

You gain expertice on Piloting while flying a Freighter

Level 3: Swindling (Either)

Using your smooth tongue, you can negotiate a 50% increase in any selling price, or a 25% decrease in buying price.

Level 7: Cloak (Starship)/h3>

You cloak your ship, making you undetectable for 3 turns.

Level 12: Overdrive (Starship)

You kick your thrusters into overdrive, doubling the ships speed for 2 turns

Level 19: Cargo Ejecting (Starship)

You rig one of your cargo shipments to explode, then launch it at the enemy. Does 6d8 explosive damage in a 100-foot radius.

Starfighter Archetype

You gain expertice on Piloting while flying a Starfighter

Level 3: Ion Blast (Starship)

You change your shot type to an ion blast, which ignores enemy shields. This does 1d8 damage and has 3 uses per long rest.

Level 7: Evasive Maneuvers (Starship)

Take evasive maneuvers, giving you advantage on your next roll, and giving the enemy disadvantage on attack rolls for the next 3 turns.

Level 12: Reactor Boost (Starship)

Increase the output from your reactor, which doubles your weapon damage for 2 turns.

Level 19: Commanding rally (Starship)

As a bonus action, encourage your squadron, giving every ship in your party, including yourself an extra action.
