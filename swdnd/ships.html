Space Ships

Ships are an important part of Star Wars D&D. They are needed to travel between planets, and can engage in space combat. There are 4 different categories of ship, categorized by the number of people they hold.

Starfighter

Capacity: 1-2 people

Damage: 1d10 damage

Armour Class: 12 AC

Hit Points: 60 HP

Speed: 40 speed

Mod Slots: 1 mod slot

Frieghter

Capacity: 1-6 people

Damage: 1d8 damage

Armour Class: 15 AC

Hit Points: 120 HP

Speed: 25 speed

Mod Slots: 3 mod slots

Corvette

Capacity: 30-100 people (5 minimum)

Damage: 1d10 damage, 3 attacks

Armour Class: 15 AC

Hit Points: 180 HP

Speed: 35 speed

Mod Slots: 6 mod slots

Destroyer

Capacity: 1000-10000 people (10 people minimim)

Damage: 2d12 damage, 6 attacks

Armour Class: 20 AC

Hit Points: 500 HP

Speed: 40 speed

Mod Slots: 20 mod slots

About Space Ships

Ship Types

Most ships from star wars will fit under one of these categories. If you are unsure of the category, find out how many people the ship you want would normally fit, or consult your dm.

Combat

Combat in space works the same as on the ground, damage, AC, and HP are all identical to ground combat. Attack rolls for space ships use your piloting skill.

Mod Slots

Mod slots are unique to ships. Each categoy of ship has a certain number of mod slots, which can each hold one mod. Mods change the stats of the ship. Different tpyes of mods can be found below.
