Mandalorian

Bound by creed and tradition, Mandalorians are warriors of unparalleled discipline and ferocity. Masters of combat in all its forms, they adapt to any battlefield with their iconic armor, cutting-edge weaponry, and unshakable resolve. Whether defending their honor, protecting their clan, or fighting for glory, Mandalorians uphold the ancient tenet: "This is the Way."

Hit points

Hit Dice: 1d12
Hit Points at 1st level: 12 + your Constitution modifier
Hit points at Higher Levels: 1d12 (or 7) + your Constitution modifier per Mandalorian level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light Armor, Medium Armor, Heavy armor, Mandalorian Armor, Energy Shields, Jetpack
Weapons: All Blasters, All Melee (Excluding Lightsabers), All Explosives
Tools: Demolition Kit
Saving throws: Strength, Constitution
Skills: Choose 3 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, Perception, Religion, Survival

Equipment

Choose between Whistling Birds x15 or Jetpack, DE-10 blaster pistol, Vibroblade, choose any one armor piece to be made of Beskar, One set of medium Mandalorian armor. 100 credits.

Ability Score Improvments

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2 or increase 2 ability scores by one. You cannot increase a skill above 20.

Skills

Level 2: Danger Sense

You gain an uncanny sense of when things nearby aren’t right, giving you an edge when you dodge away from danger. You have advantage on Dexterity Saving Throws against Effects that you can see, such as traps and Spells. You can't be Blinded, Deafened, or Incapacitated.

Level 3: Mandalorian Archetype

you chose your preferred weapon, whether that be blasters, or melee weapons. Your choice grants you features at 3rd, 5th, 12th, and 19th levels.

Level 5: Extra Attack

When you take the attack action, you may make one additional attack.

Level 9: Mandalorian Precision

You can roll one additional weapon damage die when deciding the extra damage for a critical hit with a melee Attack. This increases to 2 additional dice at 13th level and 3 additional dice at 17th level.

Level 12: Intimidating Presence

You can use your action to frighten someone with your menacing presence. When you do so, choose one creature that you can see within 30 feet of you. If the creature can see or hear you, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC equal to 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Charisma modifier) or be Frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

Level 15: Retaliation

When you take damage from a creature that is within attack range, you can use your Reaction to make an Attack against that creature.

Level 17: Strength in Numbers

While within 15 feet from your party members, you gain +1 to attack rolls per ally within range.

Level 20: This is the Way

You embody the Mandalorian Code of Honor. Your Strength, Dexterity and Constitution scores increase by 3. Your maximum for those scores is now 23.

Blaster Archetype

Level 3: Improved Critical

Your blaster attacks deal a critical strike on a 19 or 20.

Level 5: Mandalorian Athleticism

Once per long rest, you can reroll a failed saving throw, but you must take that roll.

Level 12: Superior Critical

Your blaster attacks will deal a critical strike on an 18.

Level 19: Mandalorian Conditioning

You reach the pinnacle of resilience in battle. At the start of each turn, if you are below half of your health, you regain 5 + your Constitution modifier. You do not gain this benefit if you are at 0 health.

Melee Archetype

Once every turn you may choose to use one of the following maneuvers. You can use up to 5 maneuvers per short rest. If in combat, only one maneuver may be used per turn.

Level 3: Disarming Attack

When you attack, you can try to disarm. The enemy must roll a DC 13 Strength saving throw, on a failed save, they drop their weapon at their feet.

Level 3: Distracting Strike

When you attack, make a DC 13 Dexterity Saving throw. On a successful roll, the enemy is distracted, giving advantage on the next attack roll on that target.

Level 5: Commander’s Strike

Instead of attacking, you can call an ally to action, letting use their reaction to make an attack. You can still use any extra attacks you may have.

Level 5: Power Strike

When you attack, add 1d8 to your damage roll. This upgrades to 1d12 at Lv. 18.

Level 12: Evasive Footwork

When you Move, you can roll 1d10 and add the result to your AC until your next turn.

Level 12: Parry

When an enemy hits you with a melee attack, you can use your reaction and reduce 1d8 + your Dexterity modifier from the damage.

Level 19: Riposte

When a creature misses a melee attack on you, you can use your reaction to attack it. You gain advantage on the attack roll.

Level 19: Rally

As a bonus action, you can rally your teammates. Choose one target, that target gains temporary Hp equal to a 1d10 + your Charisma modifier.
