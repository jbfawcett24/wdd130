Toydarian

Toydarians are stout, winged beings known for their shrewd nature and resilience. They prefer to stay neutral in conflicts, focusing instead on trade and self-preservation. With their natural resistance to Force manipulation and the ability to fly, Toydarians are adept at maneuvering both physically and socially. Their knack for survival and independence makes them valuable allies and formidable negotiators.

Toydarian Traits

Ability Scores

Your wisdom score increases by 2 and your constitution score increases by 1.

Age

Toydarians usually live for around 90 years.

Alignment

Toydarians favor peace, and usually avoid conflicts like choosing sides in war, so they tend toward neutral alignments.

Size

Toydarians stand at about 4ft in height.

Movement

Toydarians have a ground speed of 20 feet

Languages

Toydarians speak Basic and Huttese

Force Resistance

Toydarians brains have an unusual composition making them resistant to force manipulation and influence.

Flight

Toydarians Have a flight speed of 35 feet, but cannot fly while in heavy armor.
